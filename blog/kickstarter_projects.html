Viz: Successfully Funded Kickstarter Projects by Category

Data source: Kaggle

Notes: Original dataset includes over 378K Kickstarter projects and roughly a third of them were successfully funded. Each project falls into one of fifteen categories (as shown as dots in the plot). Four of those categories (technology, games, design, and fashion) on average, have a higher amount of overfunding (the amount raised above the goal) than the original goal of the project.
